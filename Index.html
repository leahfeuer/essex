Finishes

We can apply these finishes to just about any flat surface you have - up to 5 ft wide!

WATER BASED PRIMER

Use white primer as initial coat to build hide. It is compatible with both water base products and solvent based products. Other colors available.

OIL

True oil finishes give the wood a natural look and feel. Available clear or colors, on panels and floors.

WIRE BRUSHING

Defines grain structure and adds depth to your panel and floor. We can finish it too.

OPAQUE COLORS & STAINS

We offer stains, tints and opaque colors. Custom and stock colors available.

SUPERSEAL

Clear up your biggest bottle neck. We seal and sand your board. You apply the top coat. Compatible with solvent based finishes.

HEALTH SHIELD & LAB GRADE

FIRE SHIELD

UV TUFFSTUFF

Materials We Sell

In addition to our finishing services, we offer 2 product lines of colored wood composite panels. Viroc is composed of wood particles and cement that can be used both indoor and outdoor. Valchromat is an evolution of MDF where fibers are colored individually and uses unique bonding resins that give the panel greater load resistance. Both Viroc and Valchromat offer a color throughout panel that is easy to machine, moisture resistant, fire retardant and non-toxic. The flexibility and durability allow for various applications from kitchen counters to floors to exterior cladding to toys.
