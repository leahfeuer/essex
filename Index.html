Materials We Sell

In addition to our finishing services, we offer 2 product lines of colored wood composite panels. Viroc is composed of wood particles and cement that can be used both indoor and outdoor. Valchromat is an evolution of MDF where fibers are colored individually and uses unique bonding resins that give the panel greater load resistance. Both Viroc and Valchromat offer a color throughout panel that is easy to machine, moisture resistant, fire retardant and non-toxic. The flexibility and durability allow for various applications from kitchen counters to floors to exterior cladding.
