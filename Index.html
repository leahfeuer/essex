Finishes

We can apply these finishes to just about any flat surface you have - up to 5 ft wide!

OPAQUE COLORS & STAINS

We offer stains, tints and opaque colors. Custom and stock colors available.

OIL

True oil finishes give the wood a natural look and feel. Clear or color oil available for panels and floors.

WIRE BRUSHING

Enhances grain structure and adds depth to your panels and floors.

WATER BASED PRIMER

Use white primer as an initial coat to build hide. It is compatible with both water-based products and solvent-based products. Other colors available.

SUPERSEAL

Clear up your biggest bottle neck. We seal and sand your board. You apply the top coat. Compatible with solvent-based finishes.

HEALTH SHIELD & LAB GRADE

Perfect for hospitals, labs, schools or locker rooms. EPA certified Health Shield inhibits bacterial growth, mold and the spread of MRSA. Lab Grade is SEFA certified.

FIRE SHIELD

This fire retardant provides a Class A fire rating on plywood or lumber.

UV FINISH

Clear, durable and classic. Our standard finish saves you time and money. Available in different sheens.

UV TUFFSTUFF

Aluminum oxide makes our panel and floor finish tougher than anything you could do onsite.

Materials We Sell

In addition to our finishing services, we offer 2 product lines of colored wood composite panels. Viroc is composed of wood particles and cement that can be used both indoors and outdoors. Valchromat is an evolution of MDF where fibers are colored individually. It uses unique bonding resins that give the panel greater load resistance. Both Viroc and Valchromat offer a color throughout panel that is easy to machine, moisture-resistant, fire-retardant and non-toxic. The flexibility and durability allow for various applications. Use it for kitchen counters, floors, exterior cladding, toys and more.
